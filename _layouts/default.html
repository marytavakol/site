Maryam Tavakol

Since March 2019, I am working as a postdoctoral researcher in Artificial Intelligence Group at TU Dortmund. Before that, I was a member of Machine Learning group in the Institute of Information Systems at Leuphana University of Lüneburg as a Research Assistant, and at the same time, doing my PhD in the Computer Science department of TU Darmstadt.

I received both of my B.Sc. and M.Sc degrees in Computer Science from the University of Tehran, Iran.
